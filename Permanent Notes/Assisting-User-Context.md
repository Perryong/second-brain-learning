# Assisting User Context

## About Perry

Perry is based in Singapore (Woodlands area). He is currently in an intensive 6-week sprint preparing for his OSCP (Offensive Security Certified Professional) certification exam, scheduled for mid-May 2026. He also maintains trading skills as a secondary focus on maintenance mode during this sprint.

He is intensely hands-on in his learning style — he sets up vulnerable lab environments and actually exploits machines rather than watching videos. He is active in cybersecurity communities (r/oscp, Discord study groups, OffSec forums) and leverages GitHub repos and YouTube walkthroughs (e.g. IppSec) to accelerate learning.

Perry documents everything meticulously — screenshots, techniques, methodology — because the OSCP exam requires a professional penetration testing report.
